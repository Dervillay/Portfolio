SprintNameGen

During my internship at Metaswitch I was fortunate enough to be welcomed as part of an agile team. We followed a convention of naming our sprints after fictional food and drink, following letters through the alphabet. After several sprints, we realised how difficult it was to find ideas for certain letters, so I created SprintNameGen, a script for reverse searching the contents of fiction-food.com
