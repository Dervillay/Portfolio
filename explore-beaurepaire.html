Explore Beaurepaire - Group Project

For my second year software engineering module, I worked as part of an Agile team with 5 other students to create a mobile-friendly web-app for a local charity.

The brief was to create an interactive system allowing visitors to the Beaurepaire manor heritage site to explore the site digitally, in the aim of increasing awareness of its cultural significance. The system was designed to allow members of the charity to easily access and edit aspects of the system, including informative, interactive sections on a 3D map.

GitHub and Live System
