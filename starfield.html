StarField

Hold left click to accelerate through the void of space, or release to have a look around. Hovering over stars and planets will reveal their names (assuming object labelling is turned on).
Using the sliders and buttons below, you can customise the StarField. Press Generate to create a new instance with your chosen parameters.
Try resizing the window you're viewing this in, each StarField adapts dynamically to fill the screen.
(Note that Object Labelling will not work when the StarField is drawn onto a 3D shape)
