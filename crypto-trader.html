Algorithmic Crypto Trader

As an intellectual curiosity project, I currently work amongst a group of peers under Oxford Investment Group LLP, where we develop Python software for algorithmic cryptocurrency trading.

This entails the creation of algorithms to analyse the movement of prices in the cryptocurrency markets from previous data, using financial indicators and factors of confluence to give suggestions as to whether to buy or sell specific currencies at certain times.

These strategies are then run and executed on historic data as if the movements of the market were unknown. In doing this, the relative success of the algorithm can then be analysed, and a successful algorithm may then be deployed to run on real or demo accounts to trade actual currencies.

By doing this we hope to find profitable, consistent strategies for trading cryptocurrencies, and also to develop our general Python, version control and teamworking skills.

Unfortunately, since this project is run under an NDA, I am unable to provide examples of my code or the specifics of how it has been written, but would be more than happy to discuss my ideas and the skills developed and utilised in doing it.
